# Cookiya – Product Principles

## Product Vision

Cookiya helps people answer one simple question:

**"Aj Kya Banayein?"**

The app should make deciding what to cook quick, enjoyable and stress-free.

---

# Product Goals

- Help users discover recipes quickly.
- Prioritize recipes based on ingredients available at home.
- Reduce decision fatigue.
- Provide a clean and enjoyable cooking experience.
- Work offline whenever possible.

---

# UX Principles

## 1. Inspire First

The first thing users should see is inspiration.

The Home screen should immediately recommend something worth cooking.

---

## 2. Progressive Discovery

Information hierarchy:

1. Greeting
2. Today's Pick
3. Search
4. Categories
5. Recipe Collections
6. Recipe Detail

---

## 3. Minimal Cognitive Load

Avoid overwhelming users.

Show only what helps them make the next decision.

---

## 4. Familiar Navigation

Navigation should remain predictable and simple.

---

## 5. Accessibility

- High contrast
- Minimum 44x44 touch targets
- Readable typography
- Consistent spacing
- Clear icons

---

# Design Language

Inspired by the Julienne UI.

Use:

- Warm neutral colors
- Soft rounded corners
- Premium cooking aesthetics
- Generous spacing
- Subtle shadows
- Clean typography

---

# Home Screen Priority

The first major content after the greeting is:

## ⭐ Today's Pick

This is Cookiya's hero section.

Its purpose is to inspire users before asking them to search.

---

# Prototype Policy

The prototype is the functional source of truth.

Reuse:

- navigation
- interactions
- component behaviour

Improve:

- visual hierarchy
- accessibility
- spacing
- typography
- information hierarchy

Never modify the prototype directly.

---

# Development Rules

- Reuse components whenever possible.
- Do not duplicate components.
- Keep business logic separate from UI.
- Prefer reusable solutions over screen-specific code.
- Commit after every completed sprint.
